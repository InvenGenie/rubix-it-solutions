Manpower Consultancy

Rubix has launched its Professional Staffing Services with a commitment to help organizations find high-quality candidates within the promised time frame, both locally and nationally. Our dedicated staffing team follows a structured, methodical recruitment process that ensures every role is filled with the right talent, backed by consistent communication and measurable results. In addition to traditional staffing capabilities, Rubix integrates advanced AI-driven recruitment tools to accelerate and strengthen the hiring cycle. Our AI screening models help analyze resumes, validate skill matches, detect inconsistencies, and rank applicants based on role requirements. This allows your organization to receive a pre-vetted shortlist faster, with higher accuracy and reduced manual effort. Our process is built to support full-cycle hiring, covering sourcing, screening, assessments, interviews, and onboarding coordination. With Rubix as your staffing partner, you benefit from both experienced human recruiters and intelligent automation, resulting in a dependable hiring pipeline, improved candidate quality, and significantly shorter turnaround times.

Our Products

Nova Mymentor is our flagship AI-powered learning management system designed to teach students their academic syllabus in a personalized, engaging, and structured way. Using adaptive AI, the platform tailors lessons, quizzes, and explanations to each student’s pace while providing instant feedback and 24/7 doubt support. Nova Mymentor combines syllabus-based learning with guided homework assistance, weekly mock tests, and strong conceptual foundation-building for IIT-JEE and NEET aspirants.
